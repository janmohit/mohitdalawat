Hobbies

My father taught me how to play the game when I saw my uncle play it and asked about it. I am also a member of the DTU Chess Team. Do check out my chess profile here. I love playing chess and I enjoy watching famous chess games of various Grandmasters on youtube. My personal favourite Grandmasters are Magnus Carlsen, Bobby Fischer and Mikhail Tal.

Every year I travel to places with my friends and family to enjoy the scenic beauty of nature. I strongly believe one should spend time with nature.
